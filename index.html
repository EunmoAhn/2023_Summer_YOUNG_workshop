YOUNG Workshop

2023, July, 5-7, St.John's Hotel, Gangneung

About the workshop

This is the Yonsei Observable Universe Group (YOUNG) 2023 summer workshop, where we focus on sharing our recent research updates. The main topics of the workshop are the followings.

  • Galaxy cluster science in the era of JWST
  • Weak lensing and strong lensing analysis of galaxy clusters & cosmology
  • Implementing machine learning for the upcoming wide-field surveys
  • Numerical simulations of merging galaxy clusters
